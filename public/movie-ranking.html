The best movie according to me

My Top Three movies of all time

Twilight

A teenage girl falls in love with a mysterious vampire, leading to a passionate yet dangerous romance. Their love faces challenges from both supernatural foes and the complexities of their worlds.

Predestination

In a complex time-travel tale, a temporal agent embarks on a mission to stop a terrorist, only to uncover a profound connection to his own past. As he navigates the paradoxes of time, he confronts the blurred lines between fate and free will.

Roja

"Roja" tells the poignant story of a young woman from a small village who falls in love with a government official. Their romance is tested when he is kidnapped by militants, leading her on a desperate journey to save him. The film addresses themes of love, sacrifice, and the harsh realities of political conflict, all set against a backdrop of beautiful music and stunning visuals.
